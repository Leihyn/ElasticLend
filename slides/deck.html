The Problem

$200K in collateral. Same value. Very different risk.

Alice: Concentrated
$200K
100% in ETH across two chains. Looks diversified. ETH crashes 50%, she loses $100K. Underwater.
Bob: Diversified
$200K
Split across ETH, BTC, USDC, LINK on four chains. ETH crashes 50%, he loses $25K. Still healthy.

Every lending protocol gives them similar borrowing power. This is wrong.

The Research

Elastic Restaking Networks

Roi Bar-Zur and Ittay Eyal, ACM CCS 2025

Elastic networks exhibit superior robustness compared to previous approaches, and demonstrate a synergistic effect where an elastic restaking network enhances its blockchain's security.

Corollary 1: Elastic networks are strictly more expressive than atomic ones.
