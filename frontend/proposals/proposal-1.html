Rigid lending
breaks portfolios.
Elastic bends.

Traditional DeFi lending treats every dollar of risk as independent. Drop ETH 50% and BTC 45% simultaneously? Rigid protocols subtract both losses in full, ignoring that correlated assets rarely hit worst-case together. ElasticLend applies portfolio theory: your borrowing power is collateral minus the square root of the sum of squared expected losses, not the raw sum. The result: more capital efficiency when diversified, same safety margin when concentrated.
